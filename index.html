Purpose of the Housing Construction Corporation

The Housing Construction Corporation is dedicated to providing affordable, high-quality housing options to citizens and promoting sustainable urban development. We aim to enhance the quality of life by offering safe, secure, and modern housing solutions.

Housing Allocation Information

The Housing Construction Corporation offers various types of housing based on need and eligibility. Applicants are evaluated based on criteria such as income, family size, and community involvement. Successful applicants will be notified by email and official letter.
